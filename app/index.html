All about us

  • At LinkCut, we believe in the power of simplicity

    In a world where businesses are constantly battling for attention, long, unwieldy URLs can be a roadblock to engagement. That’s why we’ve made it our mission to streamline the digital experience by transforming long, complex links into short, easy-to-share URLs that help businesses stand out.

  • Founded with the goal of simplifying the digital landscape, Link Cut offers more than just URL

    We provide businesses with powerful tools to track and optimize their links, giving them insights into customer behavior and engagement. Our platform is built for businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises, who want to improve their online.

  • We’re here to make your digital life easier, one short link at a time

    Whether you're sharing a link on social media, in a marketing campaign, or in your internal communications, LinkCut ensures that your URLs are clean, professional, and tailored to your needs

Our Services

  • Custom URL
    Shortening

    Link Analytics
